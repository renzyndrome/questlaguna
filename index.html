WHO WE ARE

RED TAG stands for Reborn Embrace Defeat: The Ambitious Ground which challenge all who want to win the fight of trusting God's promise.

In hospitals, REDTAG status symbolizes the fight for death and life. Death is the battle, NEW LIFE is the promise. RED TAG event promises to empower and equip anyone who wants to embrace God's calling.

QUEST LAGUNA is a promise-driven church that is committed to win souls and make Christ-like disciples by following Jesus' leadership. Through evangelism, Quest Laguna's number increased throughout the year encompassing San Pedro, Biñan, Sta. Rosa and even GMA, Cavite and by continuing faithfullness, the whole Laguna area.

Our passion is to love God and love His people.
"At ang apoy na ito na naglalagablab sa puso namin ang syang apoy na tatalo sa apoy ng impyerno."
